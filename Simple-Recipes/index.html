Deep Dish Skillet Pizza

Prep Time:

30-40 minutes

Level:

medium

Deep dish pizza has got a lot going for it: a thick crust that's tender and chewy on the inside, but nicely crisped on the outside; a little extra room for piling on the toppings; and, deep dish is less needy than its thin crust style counterpart, which requires some care when rolling and stretching. Here, you just push the dough into the skillet and you're cooking. Save the leftovers for lunch!

About

Simple Recipes celebrates the power of food to nourish, connect, and strengthen our communities. We share approachable, wholesome recipes that make cooking feel natural—whether yoU are just starting out or already confident in the kitchen. Every dish is tested by real home cooks, built around everyday ingredients, and designed to support a healthier, more joyful way of eating. Here, good food isn’t just a meal—it’s a shared experience. Welcome to a community where cooking brings us together. Let's make something meaningful.
